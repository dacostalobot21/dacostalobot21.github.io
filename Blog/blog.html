Student Life at SPP

Being a student at Saint Peter's Prep, I am expected to be involved one way or another. Besides academic perfection (something I strive for daily),
I love to practice music (concert band and choir), historical knowledge (history bowl club), chess club, and I'm thinking about now joining the coding club!
As a scholar, many expect me to have an overwhelming workload of difficult classes, and I'll admit, it's going to be very difficult. My classes include
AP World History, Honors Pre-Calculus, Honors English II, Honors Chemistry, Honors Latin II, Spanish I, and The New Testament (sophomore religion course).

Life Outside of School

Although I am greatly involved at SPP (as all should be, I believe), I'm also rather involved in my hometown of Nutley, New Jersey.
